Binging with Babish

Binging with Babish is a youtube channel focused on emulating food from various forms of media into reality. The host Andrew Rea is a very talented chef who attempts to recreate the dishes with extreme accuracy, but what makes this channel unique is that after the accurate recreation, Andrew takes it upon himself to recreate an improved version. Andrew also films a series where he teaches kitchen basics with a good twist of dry humor, pack that along with some amazing cinematography and an impressive show of culinary skills and you get a truly unique experience.
